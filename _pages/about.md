I am a **Postdoc** at the **University of Oxford**, where I collaborate with [Prof. Paul Goldberg](https://www.cs.ox.ac.uk/people/paul.goldberg/index1.html) on path following algorithms and the dynamics of no-regret learning. 

### Academic Journey

I earned my Ph.D. from the **University of Edinburgh**, under the supervision of [Prof. Richard Mayr](https://homepages.inf.ed.ac.uk/rmayr/) and [Prof. Kousha Etessami](https://homepages.inf.ed.ac.uk/kousha/). My doctoral research focused on turn-based stochastic games and Markov Decision Processes (MDPs), specifically investigating systems with multiple objectives. During my Ph.D., I gained practical experience as an intern with the J.P.Morgan Time Series and Reinforcement Learning (TSRL) team.
